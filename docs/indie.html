Why do they need it?
Distraction and poor prioritisation kill output; they need a simple, frictionless system

How do I find users?
Write **SEO content** targeting the core pain-point keyword. Engage in niche Discord / Slack / Reddit communities where your target users hang out. Cold-DM 10–20 potential users on Twitter/LinkedIn and offer free beta access.
