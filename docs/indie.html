OpenFate AI
OpenFate AI helps you understand who you are, why you struggle, and what your birth chart reveals about love, wealth, career, and your next steps.
toolify Tech 2/5Acquisition 5/5Revenue 3/5Indie 5/5 Visit Product →
15
/ 20
Who are the users?
**Spirituality enthusiasts and people curious about fortune telling or astrology** (B2C — individual users making a personal purchase decision).
Why do they need it?
They want personalised spiritual guidance or entertainment without expensive readings
What the product says: _"OpenFate AI helps you understand who you are, why you struggle, and what your birth chart reveals about love, wealth, career, and your next steps"_
How does it find users?
**Viral sharing** is the engine — users share their reading results on social media. Target SEO keywords like 'free tarot reading online', 'daily horoscope [zodiac]'. TikTok/Reels short demos can explode in the spirituality niche.
Does it make money? How much?
Free with credits / microtransactions, or freemium with a **$2.99–$4.99/month** 'unlimited readings' tier. This audience converts well on impulse micro-purchases. Ad-supported free tier also viable at scale.
What can I learn from this?
- Spiritual / entertainment niches are vastly underserved by developers — most builders ignore them, leaving them wide open. - Users come back *daily* if the content feels personalised and fresh — retention is built-in if you add variety (daily draws, moon phases, etc.).
One-sentence pitch
_OpenFate AI helps spirituality enthusiasts and people curious about fortune telling or astrology by letting them openfate ai helps you understand who you are, why you struggle, and what your birth chart reveals about love, wealth, career, and your next steps._
Can I build it?
**Yes** — low technical complexity. A solo developer could ship an MVP in 1–2 weeks using existing APIs and a simple UI.
How do I find users?
Post on **r/astrology**, **r/tarot**, **r/spirituality** — these are massive, active communities hungry for tools. Create TikTok videos showing a live reading. Target '[zodiac sign] daily horoscope' SEO — millions of monthly searches.
💡 First Step: Check Google Trends for 'free tarot reading online' and 'daily horoscope'. If trending, build a dead-simple landing page with ONE free reading — no login required — and post the link on r/astrology tonight.
#2
React Developer Tools
Adds React debugging tools to the Chrome Developer Tools.
chrome_extensions Tech 1/5Acquisition 4/5Revenue 2/5Indie 5/5 Visit Product →
12
/ 20
Who are the users?
**Software developers and engineers** (B2C prosumer / B2B small teams).
Why do they need it?
Developer workflow has constant friction — this removes one specific bottleneck
What the product says: _"Adds React debugging tools to the Chrome Developer Tools"_
How does it find users?
**Chrome Web Store** organic search is the primary channel — users search for a task and your extension appears. Submit to extension directories (extlib.io, alternativeto). Zero ongoing CAC once listed.
Does it make money? How much?
Monetisation model not explicit from public info. For indie viability, target freemium with a **$9–$29/month** paid tier. Validate willingness to pay before building the billing system.
What can I learn from this?
- The Chrome Web Store is a search engine most developers forget to compete in — a well-optimised listing gets passive, recurring installs with zero ad spend. - Extensions have structurally better retention than web apps: uninstalling requires deliberate effort, so users stick around.
One-sentence pitch
_React Developer Tools helps software developers and engineers by letting them adds react debugging tools to the chrome developer tools._
Can I build it?
**Yes** — Chrome extensions are HTML/CSS/JS. Any web developer can ship an MVP in a weekend. Google's Manifest V3 docs are excellent.
How do I find users?
Optimise your **Chrome Web Store listing** (title + description for search terms). Post in subreddits relevant to the use case. Submit to ProductHunt and extension directories (extlib.io, chromeextensions.dev).
💡 First Step: Search the Chrome Web Store for similar extensions and read the **1-star reviews** — that's your feature backlog. Build one clear improvement over the top competitor and launch.
